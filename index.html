HELLO!

I am Becky, a twenty-two year old web developer and designer. About six year ago I fell in love with graphic design while learning to design brochures, magazine covers and business cards. A year later I was introduced to web design, creating web pages and small websites this became the turning point in my educational career. After high school I decided to pursue my love of web and graphic design at Pennsylvania College of Technology, to then receive a Bachelors of Science in Web and Interactive Media Design as of May 2016. When I'm not busy designing, I enjoy hunting, fishing and camping, if it involves being outside I'm probably doing it.
